Color & Composition

Where Art
Informs Design

Painting sharpens my sensitivity to color, composition, and mood — principles I carry into every screen I design.

Every stroke on canvas teaches me about balance, contrast, and emotional resonance. When I choose a color palette for an app or decide where to place a CTA, I'm drawing from the same intuition that guides my brush. Painting is meditation and practice — it keeps my visual language fresh and intentional.

From abstracts to figurative work, each piece informs how I approach visual hierarchy and emotional design.
